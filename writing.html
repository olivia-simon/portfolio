The Art of Writing

“There is nothing to writing. All you do is sit down at a typewriter and bleed.”
― Ernest Hemingway

While I don't advocate pre-writing sacrificial blood-shedding, Hemingway did capture the fundamental paradox of writing - we have to give all of ourselves to our writing, or we have nothing at all.


Well, I'm here to give all of myself in my writing to make your content better. I'd love to join forces with your writing staff, blogging team, or work freelance for you. My philosphy is to write efficiently, concisely, but with a sense of humor (and maybe a little less blood than Hemmingway would suggest).


Check out some of the articles I've written below for a sampling of my work for forknplate.com and writing style.
